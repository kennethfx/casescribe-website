FAQ

Common questions.

Is using CaseScribe compliant with IEA requirements?
CaseScribe is a productivity tool — it helps Employment Mentors draft case notes faster and track compliance requirements. WAOP remains the system of record for all IEA compliance obligations. Notes generated by CaseScribe should be reviewed, edited as needed, and saved to your system of record as usual. The platform is built around IEA v1.1.4 guidelines, including servicing requirements, outcome fee rules, and the Targeted Compliance Framework — but it does not replace any mandatory recording obligations under the Deed.
Is CaseScribe an app I need to install?
No installation required. CaseScribe runs entirely in your web browser — on desktop, tablet, or mobile. Just go to app.casescribe.com.au, log in, and you're ready to use it. Nothing to install, no IT approval required for individual access.
Can it work alongside my current CRM or case management system?
Yes. CaseScribe works alongside your existing tools — WAOP, your organisation's CRM, SharePoint, or any other system. It does not replace them. The typical workflow is: use CaseScribe to generate and review a case note, then copy it into your system of record. Most EMs find this takes seconds. Native integrations with specific CRMs are on the roadmap for future development.
How accurate are the AI-generated notes?
CaseScribe generates notes based on what you provide — the more detail in your rough notes or voice recording, the more accurate the output. The AI understands IEA terminology, templates, and compliance language. Templates are generic and IEA-compliant out of the box. If your organisation uses a specific note format, paste it into the template customiser — CaseScribe will follow your exact headings from that point on. Every note should be reviewed before saving. Think of it as a highly capable first draft that you refine — not a finished document you accept without reading.

Pricing will be per seat per month, designed to be accessible for individual EMs and scalable for teams. We will give pilot users advance notice before any paid plans begin, and early access partners will receive preferential pricing. To discuss team pricing, book a demo.
Can our whole team use it?
Yes. CaseScribe supports Employment Mentors, In Work Mentors, Employer Engagement Specialists, and Team Leaders — each with role-specific templates and features. Team Leaders get a dedicated dashboard showing caseload health, compliance status, and outcome pipeline across their team. For team onboarding, book a demo and we'll set your team up together.
